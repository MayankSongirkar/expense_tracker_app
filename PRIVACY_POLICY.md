Smart Expense Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Smart Expense Tracker (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide
When you use our App, you may provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

- **Expense Data**: Transaction amounts, descriptions, categories, dates, and notes
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, features accessed, and interaction data

### Automatically Collected Information
We may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage of the App:

- **Device Information**: Hardware model, operating system, unique device identifiers

- **Technical Data**: IP address (if applicable), browser type, and technical specifications

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### Primary Functions
- **Expense Tracking**: Store and manage your personal expense data locally on your device
- **Analytics**: Generate spending insights, trends, and category breakdowns
- **PDF Generation**: Create expense receipts and reports for your records
- **Data Export**: Allow you to export your data in CSV format
- **App Functionality**: Provide core features like monthly reset and historical data access

### App Improvement
- **Performance Monitoring**: Identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
- **Feature Development**: Understand usage patterns to improve existing features
- **User Experience**: Enhance app usability and interface design

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Primary Storage**: All your expense data is stored locally on your device using Hive database
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your personal expense data on our servers
- **Device Security**: Data security depends on your device's security measures

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard methods
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to any data that may be collected for app improvement
- **Regular Updates**: Security patches and updates are provided regularly
- **Local Processing**: All expense calculations and analytics are processed locally

### Data Retention
- **Local Data**: Your expense data remains on your device until you delete it
- **App Uninstall**: All local data is removed when you uninstall the app
- **Manual Deletion**: You can delete individual expenses or clear all data at any time
